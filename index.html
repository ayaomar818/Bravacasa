The stages of concept, design and construction phase

Concept Phase includes

1

Briefing

We well send out our team to listen to your requirment and compare it with the challenges of the site and how best to deliver your design based on an in-depth site review.

2

Conceptual Design

Our team will work with you to review your budget and finalize your layout to creat conceptional plans. Based on these plans, we can provide a project estimate and timeline.

3

Mood

Our team will suggest the theme and the mood of your design and give you the chance to comment and compare with budget.

2

Work Drawings

After confirming everything and getting approvals working drawings will be produced to commence construction.

3

Build up

After all approvals and confirmations, work will start to realize the aristic work that was done.
